Planning notes

  • Intro Statistics carries the most planned additions (+): effect size, NNT/NNH, and clinical vs. statistical significance all belong here and are currently absent from the entire curriculum.
  • Outbreak session is the natural home for ARR/RRR (alongside RR/OR) and correlation vs. causation (in the context of observational study interpretation).
  • Diagnostic Testing session should integrate AI/algorithmic literacy — the conceptual bridge from Sn/Sp and AUC to clinical decision support tools is direct.
  • Epi Rev (Pulm) — one hour in the Pulmonology module. Scope to be determined; use edit mode to assign topics.
  • Morb/Mort/Prognosis source file not in folder — content inferred from review LOs. Cross-sectional study design is a natural addition.
  • Statistical power (!) is referenced in Intro Stats but not a named learning objective and not assessed. Should be elevated to full coverage.
  • Likelihood ratios & ROC/AUC (!!) are recommended-only handouts in Diagnostic Testing. Should be elevated to required given board relevance and AI literacy connection.
